# Privacy Policy for Cozy Cabin

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

Cozy Cabin (the "Extension") is a browser extension that replaces the new tab page with a cozy, customizable experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

## 1. Information We Collect

The Extension does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or billing information.

The Extension may store certain preferences locally in your browser to provide its features, including:

- Custom quick links
- Selected default search engine
- Ambient sound settings (for example, whether fireplace, rain, or lo-fi sounds are enabled)

These preferences are stored locally on your device using browser storage and are not sent to us.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information stored locally is used only to:

- Remember your customization choices
- Keep your preferred quick links available
- Preserve your selected search engine and sound preferences
- Provide the intended user experience of the Extension

The Extension does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

## 3. Search and Third-Party Services

When you use the built-in search feature, your search query is sent to the search engine you selected (such as Google, Bing, or DuckDuckGo). The Extension itself does not collect or store those search queries.

The Extension may also open links to external websites when you click quick links or other content. Those third-party websites have their own privacy practices, and the Extension is not responsible for them.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

User preferences are stored locally in your browser and remain on your device until you clear your browser data, remove the Extension, or reset the settings.

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

The Extension does not use cookies, advertising trackers, analytics tools, or other tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under the age of 13, and it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Your Choices

You can manage or remove the Extension’s stored settings by:

- Clearing your browser’s site data
- Removing the Extension from your browser
- Resetting the Extension’s settings from the interface, if available

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this document.

## 9. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the extension’s support channel or project repository.

By using Cozy Cabin, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
